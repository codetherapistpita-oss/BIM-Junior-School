Galaxy Campus — educating children from Day Care through Junior 3 with excellence, care, and purpose.

Our Story

Building Bright Futures at Galaxy Campus

BIM Junior School Galaxy Campus was founded with a clear vision: to provide quality education from Day Care all the way to Junior 3, rooted in our motto — Nurturing Hearts and Minds.

Our school colours — purple, blue, and white — reflect who we are. White dominates our uniform (60%), symbolising purity and excellence, while purple and blue represent wisdom, trust, and ambition.

  • Day Care through Junior 3 under one roof
  • Child-centred, values-based curriculum
  • Safe, modern Galaxy Campus facilities
  • Strong partnership with parents

Programs

All Class Levels — Day Care to Junior 3

Each programme is tailored to support growth at every stage of your child's journey.

Day Care

A safe, nurturing environment with play-based routines, sensory activities, and early social development for our youngest learners.

Nursery

Introduction to structured learning through songs, stories, colours, shapes, and guided play activities.

Kindergarten (KG)

Building literacy, numeracy, and fine motor skills through creative, hands-on learning experiences.

Junior 1

Strengthening reading, writing, and mathematics foundations with growing independence and responsibility.

Junior 2

Advanced academics, critical thinking, and leadership opportunities within a supportive classroom community.

Junior 3

School readiness with comprehensive academics, exam preparation, and character development for the next chapter.
